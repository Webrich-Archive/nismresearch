# NISM Certification Exams - Comprehensive Research

**Source:** National Institute of Securities Markets (NISM) - https://www.nism.ac.in/
**Research Date:** April 7, 2026

NISM is a public trust established by the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI). It conducts certification examinations for professionals working in the Indian securities markets. All exams are computer-based MCQ format with certificates valid for 3 years (unless noted otherwise).

## Key Patterns & Observations

### Exam Tiers by Complexity
- **Entry Level:** Series XII (Foundation), V-B (MF Foundation), XXIV (AML/CFT), XXV-A (Research Sales), IFSCA-01 - lower passing marks, no negative marking
- **Standard:** Most exams - 100 questions, 2 hours, 60% passing, 25% negative marking
- **Advanced:** Series X-A, X-B, XIII, XIX-C, XXI-B - 150 marks, 3 hours, caselet-based questions, higher fees

### Negative Marking Categories
- **No negative marking:** Series II-A, II-B, V-A, V-B, X-C, XII, XXIV, XXV-A, IFSCA-01
- **10% negative marking:** Series XIX-A, XIX-B, XXI-A (AIF/PMS distributors)
- **25% negative marking:** All remaining exams (derivatives, compliance, merchant banking, managers, etc.)

### Fee Structure
- **Rs. 750:** Series XXIV (AML/CFT), IFSCA-01 - shortest/simplest exams
- **Rs. 1,200:** Series V-B (MF Foundation) - restricted eligibility
- **Rs. 1,500:** Most standard exams
- **Rs. 1,770:** Series III-C, XII, XIX-A, XIX-B
- **Rs. 2,500:** Series X-C (Renewal)
- **Rs. 3,000:** Series X-B, XIII, XIX-C, XXI-B (advanced exams)

### AIF Exam Family (XIX Series)
| Exam | Purpose | Scope |
|------|---------|-------|
| XIX-A | **Distributors** of Cat I & II AIFs | Sales/distribution |
| XIX-B | **Distributors** of Cat III AIFs | Sales/distribution |
| XIX-C | **Managers** of all AIF categories | Comprehensive management |
| XIX-D | **Managers** of Cat I & II AIFs | Category-specific management |
| XIX-E | **Managers** of Cat III AIFs | Category-specific management |

XIX-C is the comprehensive managers exam; XIX-D and XIX-E are category-specific subsets with shorter duration and lower fees.

### Common Features Across All Exams
- All certificates valid for **3 years** (except IFSCA-01: 2 years)
- **PAN card is mandatory** for certificate issuance
- All exams are **computer-based MCQ format** (except XXV-A: internet-based remotely proctored)
- **CPE (Continuing Professional Education)** programs available for revalidation
- Registration portal: https://cert.nism.ac.in/

### Study Material Notes
- **Series XIII (Common Derivatives)** does not have its own workbook; study material comprises the workbooks of Series I, IV, and VIII
- **Series XXV-A** is newly launched (Feb 2026) and may not yet have a published workbook
