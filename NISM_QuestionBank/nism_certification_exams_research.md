## 1. NISM-Series-XIII: Common Derivatives Certification Examination

| Parameter | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Series** | XIII |
| **Full Name** | NISM-Series-XIII: Common Derivatives Certification Examination |
| **Total Questions** | 150 (all MCQs, 1 mark each) |
| **Total Marks** | 150 |
| **Duration** | 3 hours |
| **Passing Marks** | 90 (60%) |
| **Negative Marking** | 25% of marks assigned per question (i.e., 0.25 marks deducted per wrong answer) |

| **Validity** | 3 years from date of exam |
| **Eligibility** | Persons associated with equity derivatives, currency derivatives, and interest rate derivatives segments |

**Syllabus Units & Weightage:**
1. Basics of Derivatives - 6 marks (4%)
2. Introduction to the Underlying Markets - 24 marks (16%)
3. Introduction to Forwards and Futures - 20 marks (13%)
4. Strategies Using Futures - 24 marks (16%)
5. Introduction to Options - 22 marks (15%)
6. Option Trading Strategies - 4 marks (3%)
7. Introduction to Trading, Clearing, Settlement & Risk Management - 25 marks (17%)
8. Legal and Regulatory Environment - 14 marks (9%)
9. Accounting and Taxation - 6 marks (4%)
10. Sales Practices, Code of Conduct and Investor Protection Measures - 5 marks (3%)
