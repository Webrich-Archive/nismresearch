## 1. NISM Series VI: Depository Operations Certification Examination

**Full Name:** NISM-Series-VI: Depository Operations Certification Examination

### Exam Structure
| Parameter | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Total Questions | 100 |
| Total Marks | 100 (1 mark each) |
| Duration | 2 hours |
| Passing Marks | 60% (60 marks) |
| Negative Marking | Yes - 25% of marks assigned per question |
| Mode | Computer-based (online at test centers) |

### Fees
Rs. 1,500/- (plus applicable taxes/payment gateway charges)

### Validity
3 years from the date of examination. Can be revalidated for 3 years via CPE program.

### Eligibility
Associated persons engaged or employed by a registered depository participant in:
- Dealing or interacting with clients
- Dealing with securities of clients
- Handling redressal of investor grievances
- Internal control or risk management
- Activities having a bearing on operational risk
- Maintenance of books and records

| 6 | Functions of DP - Dematerialisation | 8% |

| 9 | Functions of DP - Corporate Action | 5% |
| 10 | Functions of DP - Public Issues | 5% |
| 11 | Functions of DP - Debt and Government Securities | 10% |

## 6. NISM Series X-B: Investment Adviser (Level 2) Certification Examination

**Full Name:** NISM-Series-X-B: Investment Adviser (Level 2) Certification Examination

### Exam Structure
| Parameter | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Total Questions | 120 (90 independent MCQs + 6 caselets with 5 questions each) |
| Total Marks | 150 (90 MCQs x 1 mark + 30 caselet questions x 2 marks) |
| Duration | 3 hours |
| Passing Marks | 60% (90 marks out of 150) |
| Negative Marking | Yes - 25% of marks assigned per question |
| Mode | Computer-based (online at test centers) |

### Fees
Rs. 3,000/- (plus applicable taxes/payment gateway charges)

### Validity
3 years from the date of examination. Can be revalidated via CPE program or X-C renewal exam.

### Eligibility
- Individual investment adviser registered under SEBI (Investment Advisers) Regulations, 2013
- Principal officer of non-individual investment adviser
- Persons associated with investment advice

### Syllabus Units (20 Units)
Key modules include:

**Insurance (Units I-II)**
- Basics of Insurance: principles, concepts, role in personal finance
- Life Insurance Products

**Retirement Planning (Units V-VI)**
- Retirement products: accumulation and distribution
- Portfolio creation for retirement, advisor's role

**Taxation (Units VII-XI)**
- Tax framework and key concepts
- Income, residential status, five heads of income
- Clubbing, set-off and carry forward of losses
- Exempt incomes, deductions, rebates
- Taxation of debt and equity products
- DTAA provisions

**Investment Analysis**
- Analysis of equity and debt instruments
- Commodity and real estate investment
- Alternative investments

**Behavioral Finance (Unit XVII)**
- Role of emotions in goal setting
- Nudging investors, managing client emotions

**Risk Profiling (Unit XVIII)**
- Risk profiling approach and parameters
- Role in asset allocation

**Product Comparison (Unit XIX) and Case Studies (Unit XX)**
