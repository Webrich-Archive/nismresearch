# Verified Tax Rates for MF Questions — FY 2025-26 (AY 2026-27)

**Verified on:** April 8, 2026
**Sources:** [Finnovate](https://www.finnovate.in/learn/blog/mutual-fund-taxation-india-fy-2025-26), [IndiaTaxTools](https://indiataxtools.com/blog/tax-on-mutual-funds-2026-ltcg-stcg-dividend-tax-guide/), [Mirae Asset Tax Reckoner](https://www.miraeassetmf.co.in/docs/default-source/default-document-library/tax-reckoner-2025--26.pdf)

## Equity-Oriented Funds (>=65% equity)
| Type | Holding | Rate |
|------|---------|------|
| STCG | <=12 months | 20% (u/s 111A) |
| LTCG | >12 months | 12.5% (u/s 112A) above Rs. 1.25 lakh/year |

## Debt Funds (<35% equity, purchased on/after 1 Apr 2023)
| Type | Holding | Rate |
|------|---------|------|
| Any | Any period | Slab rate (always STCG, no indexation) |

## Debt Funds (<35% equity, purchased BEFORE 1 Apr 2023)
| Type | Holding | Rate |
|------|---------|------|
| STCG | <=24 months | Slab rate |
| LTCG | >24 months | 12.5% without indexation |

## Gold Funds / International Funds / FOFs
| Type | Holding | Rate |
|------|---------|------|
| STCG | <=24 months | Slab rate |
| LTCG | >24 months | 12.5% without indexation |

## Hybrid Funds
- Equity-oriented (>=65% equity): Same as equity funds
- Debt-oriented (<65% equity): Same as debt funds
- Arbitrage funds: Treated as equity funds

## ELSS
- 3-year lock-in (always LTCG)
- LTCG: 12.5% above Rs. 1.25 lakh
- Section 80C deduction up to Rs. 1.5 lakh

## Dividend (IDCW)
- Added to investor's total income, taxed at slab rate
- TDS: 10% if dividend exceeds Rs. 5,000/year

## STT
- 0.001% on equity MF redemption/sale
- Not applicable on debt fund transactions

## Cess & Surcharge
- Health & Education Cess: 4% on total tax
- Surcharge: Based on income slab
